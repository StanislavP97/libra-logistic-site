About Us

Libra Logistic srl has been operating in the field of international transportation since 2005. Over this time, we have become a reliable partner for dozens of companies in Ukraine, Moldova, and across Europe — those who value timely and cost-efficient cargo delivery.

Groupage shipments — our core expertise. We provide flexible logistics solutions ranging from express deliveries of 50 kg to full truckloads up to 24 tons.

Own vehicle fleet — we operate independently, without relying on intermediaries or forwarding agents.

Turnkey transportation — we save our clients time and money by consolidating shipments from multiple senders into one vehicle or warehouse, and by handling all necessary documentation.

Carrier’s liability insurance — All operations of company are covered by CMR insurance with a liability limit of up to 300 000 €. Check our insurance

Entrust your cargo to us — and see how logistics can be simple, transparent, and predictable.

Our Services

Libra Logistic srl specializes in international road freight transportation from Europe to Moldova and Southern Ukraine — and in the opposite direction as well. Our main area of expertise is groupage (consolidated) shipments.


We offer:

Groupage and full truckload deliveries — from small batches of goods to full loads, with door-to-door service from the sender’s warehouse directly to the recipient.

Transportation of dangerous goods (ADR) — most hazard classes, in full compliance with international regulations and complete safety control.

Preparation of transport and customs documents — including CMR, TIR, T1, EX1, EUR1, and other accompanying papers.

Consolidation and warehousing services — delivery of goods from multiple suppliers to a single warehouse for storage and repackaging.

Cargo tracking — we provide GPS monitoring of trucks and routes in real time.

Our goal is to ensure reasonable costs, cargo safety, and the shortest delivery times for every client.

Transport

For cargo transportation, we use our own fleet of modern vehicles and cooperate with reliable regional carriers. This allows us to choose the right transport for any task — from express delivery of small shipments to the transportation of oversized cargo.


Own fleet

  • Euro 6 class trucks with a load capacity of up to 24 tons.
  • Semi-trailers with a volume of up to 92 m³ and a height of up to 2.75 m.
  • Capacity up to 33 Euro pallets (1.2 × 0.8 m).
  • Side, top, and rear loading available.

Flexible solutions

Along with standard trucks, we use light-duty vehicles for express deliveries from 50 kg to 3 tons.


Safety and control

Frequently Asked Questions
1. How fast do you deliver cargo from Europe to Ukraine or Moldova?
Delivery time: depends on the country of departure and typically ranges from 3 to 12 days. For details, see the "Directions".
2. Can I send a small shipment instead of a full truckload?
Yes, you pay only for the space your cargo occupies.
3. Do you handle dangerous goods (ADR)?
Yes. We hold ADR certificates and have the required equipment to transport most hazard classes.
4. What does the “door-to-door” service include?
We collect the cargo from the sender’s warehouse, prepare all necessary documents, and deliver it directly to the recipient’s warehouse.
5. How do you ensure cargo safety?
All our transportation is covered by CMR carrier’s liability insurance. In addition, all vehicles are equipped with GPS systems for route monitoring.
6. How can I calculate the shipping cost?
Submit a request via the online form or contact our manager. We’ll prepare an offer with the optimal route and rate for your cargo.
💡 The more detailed the cargo information (weight, volume, route, timing), the more accurate the cost estimate will be.
7. What are the advantages of groupage (consolidated) shipments?
  1. Lower transportation costs
  2. More frequent deliveries
  3. Flexibility — you can send small batches without waiting for a full load
8. Why choose Libra Logistic?
  1. Optimal routes and costs We build logistics without unnecessary kilometers and intermediaries.
  2. Over 15 years of experience. Established processes, knowledge of route specifics and customer requirements.
  3. Reputation and trust. Regular partners and recommendations confirm the stability of our work. "Our clients"
9. Can several shipments be combined in one truck?
Yes. We consolidate goods from different suppliers in a single warehouse, helping reduce costs and optimize delivery.
10. Which documents do you handle?
We take care of most of the paperwork — preparing CMR, EUR1, EX1, TIR, T1, and other accompanying documents.
11. Can I track the shipment?
Yes. All our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking systems that allow real-time route monitoring.
12. What types of cargo do you not transport?
We do not accept shipments from private individuals. For details, please contact our manager.
13. Do you transport oversized cargo?
For oversized cargo, we recommend contacting specialized companies.
14. Do you provide temperature-controlled (refrigerated) transportation?
For temperature-controlled cargo, we recommend contacting specialized carriers.
15. What documents are required to send cargo?
The minimum required documents are an invoice and CMR. Depending on the route and type of cargo, additional documents and certificates may be required—we'll help you prepare everything you need.
16. How does your document flow with clients work?
  1. Starting cooperation: We sign a contract, provide a certificate of beneficial ownership, and other necessary documents. We also send a standard document package for your bank or finance department’s approval.
  2. Booking a shipment: We confirm the specific transport terms.
  3. Before crossing the Ukrainian border: We send a transport cost statement, invoice, and all details required by your customs broker for successful clearance.
  4. After unloading: We send a complete payment package — booking form, invoice, and act of completed works. After approval, we exchange original documents.
